Safety Stock Mosaic

The following is a heat map-style visualization of optimal average Cycle Service Levels (CSL), a measure of how much inventory is ideal to keep, for each SKU sub-category. Optimizing CSL's gives us recommended safety stock levels that minimize stock-out and inventory holding costs.

Optimal CSL's were calculated as part of a work project by minimizing the Total Relevant Cost equation's left-hand side (learn more) with SciPy. Sub-category names were replaced by the Top 100 fastest-growing e-commerce categories for confidentiality.

Why visualize this?

By highlighting where the optimal CSL is for each category, we can see how (in average), subcategories fall into one of 3 segments (CSL's below allowed minimum, medium CSL's, and CSL's approaching max.). Lowering the minimum CSL allowed could result in more savings due to finding the true optimal values for Group 1 sub-categories.
